About Me

Hi! I'm Loretta. I'm a junior front end web developer and I love rock climbing, video games, and my comic book t-shirts.

I completed a Diploma of Civil Engineering Design and then became extremely interested in web development. After self teaching with online courses for a year I decided to enroll in General Assembly's Web Development Immersive. And I loved every minute of it.

I'm passionated about learning always seeking to discover new things.

I'm a customer services specialst with 10 years of experience in management, conflict resolution, and communication.
